Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Ginger

  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cloves
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
